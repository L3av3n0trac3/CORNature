Triteleia grandiflora var. howellii

Common name
Howell's Triteleia
Family
Liliaceae
Family common name
Lily family
Blooms
March - July
Habitat
Grasslands, open shrublands
Differences compared to T. hyacinthina
Flowers white with blue midveins or blue with darker-colored midveins. Tubular part of flower is about 1 inch long.
